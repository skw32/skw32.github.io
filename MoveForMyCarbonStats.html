MoveForMyCarbon

Disclaimer:

This personal challenge is not intended to make people feel guilty for experiencing the world. For a start, it’s hard to care about something that you don’t know. This is also certainly not intended to isolate people who don’t enjoy activities like running or cycling. Since I already use Apps to track my sports, for me this challenge was a way to help me to shift my priorities away from quick, cheap, more polluting flights towards slower and probably more expensive train journeys that are less polluting AND will not take me 1000km away from my running goal! I share my challenge as a thought experiment that may be of interest to other fans of travel and endurance sports!

Overview

'MoveForMyCarbon' is a personal challenge to attempt to run/ cycle etc. the equivalent distance that I fly during the course of my lifetime. On this webpage I will try to keep a (fairly) up to date estimate of air miles (in km since that's how I normally think for running!) and also the km I have run, cycled, hiked, walked or rollerbladed. The aim is to be net neutral over the course of my lifetime... and to hopefully still be enjoying the mental, social and physical benefits of running late into my life! This started as a thought experiment on 24/04/2019 (during a pub discussion, as is often the way these things start...) but after doing the maths, it seems like this may actually be a plausible if I change my behaviour RIGHT NOW (and remain reasonably injury free!).

Slightly boring details

Sport distance tracking: Since I used to use the sport tracking app 'Endomondo' and since some activities are less easy to track the cumulative total with Strava (the app I currently use) (and also as there are only so many hours in a day), I'll split my total km into two categories. The 'static' and 'dynamic' totals. The static total will be distances from past activities recorded with Endomondo (including: running, cycling, hiking and walking) and the activities I usually cover less distance with on Strava and are less easy to find my cumulative total for (hiking, walking, rollerblading). The dynamic total will therefore just include running and cycling activities I record with Strava from now on (today being 25/04/2019). Flight distance tracking: To get my air mile estimates, I used websites for calculating the air mails for any flights I have taken in my 'adult life', which I count as being over 18 years old.
