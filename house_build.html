A Practical Guide to Building a House

(Whilst Working Full-Time)

Here I will document various stages, thought processes, decisions and/ or errors made during the process of building our home. I will also highlight certain compromises or time-saving approaches we took since during the process we were both working full-time (apart from a 3-month break Olivier took right at the start). This page is by no means exhaustive, just a place to note anything I thought was particularly noteworthy (and which I haven't already forgotten by now xD) during the process.
